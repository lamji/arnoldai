## Overview

International Marketing Group (IMG) is a pioneering global company that serves as an avenue toward financial security. It caters to individuals and families who wish to achieve financial independence. Founded in 2002 in the Philippines, IMG acts as a financial educator and distribution platform, partnering with leading licensed financial institutions.

IMG is not a bank or insurance company. It focuses on helping middle-income individuals‚Äîoften overlooked by the traditional financial industry‚Äîdevelop the capability and confidence to make critical financial decisions.

### Vision
> To build a new Financial Industry, where everyone is able to earn what the wealthy are earning by bringing the secrets of the wealthy to all.

### Mission
> To make a difference in the lives of many by creating wealth for families so there will be no family left behind.

## How IMG Works (The System)

IMG operates using a **Membership System** where each associate is an independent business owner. 

### Membership Benefits:
*   **Financial Education**: Access to workshops, seminars, and online training modules.
*   **Product Access**: Access to a broad array of financial products and services via partner institutions.
*   **Business Opportunity**: The ability to build a team and earn recurring income.

---

## Financial Products & Partnerships

IMG has agreements with leading financial services companies to provide a diversified choice of products.

### Key Partnerships & Clarifications:
*   **Diversified Choices**: IMG provides access to insurance, healthcare, and investments.
*   **Mutual Funds**: IMG does not sell mutual funds directly. **Mutual funds are sold by Rampver Financials.**
*   **Kaiser International**: IMG's primary partner for healthcare and long-term care plans.
*   **Regulatory Compliance**: Associates must be licensed/accredited by product providers (e.g., SEC, Insurance Commission) to legally sell regulated products.

## Business Opportunity & Compensation

IMG describes its marketing system as an entrepreneurial path to long-term business growth.

### Income Streams:
*   **Personal Commissions**: From direct product sales.
*   **Overrides**: From team members' production.
*   **Renewals & Bonuses**: Long-term recurring income and leadership pools.
*   **Incentives**: Recognition rewards such as travel incentives, watches, and rings.

*Earnings are strictly performance-based and depend on sales volume, team size, and licensing status. Success is driven by dedication and consistent effort.*

## Key Concepts (Financial 101)

IMG emphasizes several core wealth-building strategies:
*   **Proper Financial Foundation**: Building from the ground up (Healthcare -> Protection -> Debt Mgmt -> Emergency Fund -> Investment).
*   **BTID (Buy Term, Invest the Difference)**: A strategy for optimizing insurance and growth.
*   **The Power of Choice**: Avoiding one-size-fits-all solutions by offering multiple provider options.
*   **Wealth for All**: Bringing "the secrets of the wealthy" to everyday families.
